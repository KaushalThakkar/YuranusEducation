Tuition Fees

For undergraduate degrees, an international student can expect to pay between NZ$22,000 and $32,000.

For postgraduate degrees, fees range from about $26,000 to $37,000 with higher fees for subjects such as medicine and veterinary science.

A PhD programmer in New Zealand for an international student can cost between $6,500 to $9,000 per year for most subjects.

PhD programmers cost the same for domestic and international students.

Accommodation costs

There are several possible options for renting during a period of study in New Zealand. You could stay in student residence halls ($300 per week, on average), a shared flat ($120 per week) or a private flat ($215/ per week).

Other costs

  • Monthly Phone bill - $20
  • Internet plan - $70
  • Flat utilities (gas, electricity, water) - $100
  • Public Transport monthly pass - $150 (% Students)
  • Average meal cost - $20
  • Cinema Ticket - $15
