Tuition Fees

For international students, undergraduate fees start at around £10,000 for lecture-based courses, going up to £32,000 or more for a top undergraduate degree.

At postgraduate level, international fees for classroom-based programs starts at around £11,000 and goes up to £25,000. For laboratory-based programs, average annual fees vary from £12,000 to £27,200.

Living Expenses

Attending university in the UK is a fantastic opportunity, but it requires resources. If you’re considering studying abroad, it’s important to research the UK cost of living carefully, and then plan your budget accordingly.

We’ve created a simple guide to help you get an idea of what to think about when it comes to researching the living expenses in the UK for international students.
