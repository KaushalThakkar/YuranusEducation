Why Canada?

Quality of Life

Canadians and international students in Canada enjoy a high standard of living. It’s a great place to live and work. In fact, Canada ranks #1 in the world when it comes to quality of life. According to the World University Rankings, Canada also has 4 of the top 100 best student cities in the world.

Learn with the best and brightest

Canada is recognized worldwide for its outstanding quality of education—from elementary school to post-secondary studies. The educators are highly trained and bring diverse perspectives to the classroom. At the university level, 2 out of every 5 academics hold at least 1 international degree. And it has 11 of the top 250 universities worldwide.

Path to a promising future

Canada has researched graduate outcomes for the students—both domestic and international—for years and the outcome is that Canadian college and university students graduate with a strong earning potential. That’s because it positions them for a successful future and rewarding careers. In the last decade alone, Canada created 1.8 million new jobs for graduates.

Affordable education and living

Canada is known for having some of the lowest university tuition fees among English-speaking countries. Whatever your budget may be, you’ll find something just right for you in the diverse range of education options.

Experience like no other

Adventure awaits you in Canada! One of the greatest things about studying in Canada is the opportunity to experience a climate, culture and way of life that is beyond compare. From seeing the vibrant colors of our autumn leaves to the soft sounds of snowshoeing through the forest, each season and place has so much to offer.
